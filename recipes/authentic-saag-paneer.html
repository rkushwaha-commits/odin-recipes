Authentic Saag Paneer

Description

Saag paneer is a classic Indian dish of cooked spinach studded with cubes of fried paneer cheese. Thickened with cream or coconut milk, it's a hearty and filling vegetarian meal.

ingredients

  • 2 buches spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 bunch fenugreek leaves, roughly chopped1
  • 3 tablespoons canola oil, divided
  • 1/2 pound paneer, cubed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. bring a large sauepan of water to a boil;add spinach and fenugreek and cook until wilted, about 3 minutes. Dreain well.
  3. Transfer to a food processor and blend until finely chopped, about 5 pulses.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon canola oil in a large skillet over medium heat; add paneer cubes and fry until browned on all sieds, about 5 minutes. Transfer paneer onto a plate.
  5. Heat remaining 3 tablespoons canola oil in the same skillet over medium heat;add cumin seeds and fry until lightly toasted and aromatic, about 3 minutes.
  6. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, 4 to 5 minutes. Add garlic and ginger and stir to coat.
  7. Stri in tomato, garam masala, turmeric, and cayenne pepper; cook until tomato breaks down, stirring often, about 10 minutes.
  8. Stir in pureed spinach mixture, paneer cubes, and cream; add salt to taste.
  9. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
